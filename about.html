Learning made simple, beautiful and stress-free.

🌿 What is MindMint?

MindMint is a free educational website created to help students learn in an easy and organized way. It provides simple notes, grammar, important questions, one-shot revision, and study resources for different classes.

🎯 Our Mission

To make studying easier, more interesting, and accessible to every student.

✨ What You Will Find

  • 📚 Easy Notes
  • 📝 English Grammar
  • 📄 Previous Year Questions
  • 📖 Sample Papers
  • ⚡ One-Shot Revision
  • 🎯 Important Questions

💚 About the Creator

MindMint is created and maintained by Rashmi Rekha Shaw, a student who wants to make learning easier for everyone.
